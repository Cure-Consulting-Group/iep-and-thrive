## What to Expect

### Before the Program
1. **Discovery Call:** You've already had this — this is where we discussed your child's needs and confirmed fit.
2. **IEP Review:** We read your child's current IEP before day one. Every session is mapped to their actual annual goals.
3. **Program Alignment Summary:** You'll receive a 1-page document showing exactly how our sessions connect to your child's IEP goals.

### During the Program
- **Daily Parent Debrief:** At pickup (1:00 PM), we'll share a quick verbal debrief on your child's day.
- **Weekly Progress Reports:** Emailed every Friday as a PDF. Includes skill data, accuracy percentages, strategies used, and home practice recommendations.
- **Mid-Program Check-In:** A 15-minute call in Week 3 to discuss progress and adjust.

### After the Program
- **Final Comprehensive Report:** CSE-ready. Includes pre/post data, growth charts, progress narrative, and recommended IEP goal language for September.
- **Post-Program Survey:** Your feedback helps us improve for next year.
- **September Follow-Up:** We'll check in to see how the CSE meeting went and if there's anything else we can do.

## Behavior Expectations

We believe in **positive behavioral supports** and **trauma-informed practices**. We understand that students with learning differences often demonstrate behavior that is a communication of need, not defiance.

**Our approach:**
- Proactive strategies (visual schedules, structured routines, clear expectations)
- Positive reinforcement and relationship-based support
- Sensory breaks and self-regulation tools
- De-escalation and redirection before consequences

**If behavior becomes a safety concern:**
- We will contact you to discuss strategies and collaborate on a plan
- If persistent behavior compromises the safety or learning environment for other students, we may need to discuss alternative arrangements

## FAQ

**Q: Do you provide ESY or Related Services?**
No. IEP & Thrive is a private, supplemental educational program. We do not provide ESY, OT, PT, Speech, or any mandated services under IDEA.

**Q: Will you modify my child's IEP?**
No. We provide progress data and recommended goal language, but IEP modifications are made by your child's CSE team.

**Q: Can my child attend if they don't have an IEP?**
Students with 504 Plans or documented learning differences are welcome. We discuss fit during the discovery call.

**Q: Is transportation provided?**
No. Families are responsible for transportation (drop-off and pick-up).

**Q: What if my child has food allergies?**
We are an allergen-aware program. Please disclose all allergies on the enrollment form. Snacks are family-provided, so you control what your child eats.

**Q: What if we need to miss a day?**
Please notify us by 8:00 AM. We'll provide materials for at-home practice for missed sessions when possible.
