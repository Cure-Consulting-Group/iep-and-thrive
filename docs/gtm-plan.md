**Program:** SPED Summer Intensive
**Launch Date:** Early enrollment opens now — program runs July 7 – August 14, 2026

## 1. Positioning Statement

> **For Long Island parents of children with IEPs and learning differences** who have been denied ESY or failed to find credentialed summer support, **IEP & Thrive** is the **evidence-based summer intensive** that maps every session to your child's actual IEP goals and delivers weekly progress data you can bring to September.
>
> **Unlike** generic tutoring centers, summer camps, or private tutors charging $150+/hr, IEP & Thrive is led by a NYS-certified SPED interventionist with 8+ years in the NYC DOE system — someone who has sat at the CSE table and knows exactly what your child's IEP requires.

### One-Liner (for social/word-of-mouth)
> "The only summer program on Long Island designed around your child's actual IEP goals."

### Elevator Pitch (30 seconds)
> "IEP & Thrive is a 6-week summer intensive for students with IEPs and learning differences on Long Island. We review your child's IEP before day one, build every session around their annual goals, and send weekly progress reports you can take to September CSE meetings. Small groups of 6, led by a credentialed NYC DOE interventionist. It's what ESY should be — but isn't."

## 2. Messaging Hierarchy

### Primary Message
**"Your child's IEP goals — finally, a summer program that actually delivers on them."**

### Supporting Value Props

| Value Prop | Proof Point |
|-----------|-------------|
| **IEP-goal aligned instruction** | Pre-program IEP review; every session maps to annual goals; CSE-ready final report |
| **Credentialed, not generic** | NYS-certified SPED teacher; 8+ years NYC DOE; OG-trained; not a college student tutor |
| **Data parents can use** | 6 weekly progress reports (PDF); final comprehensive report with skill-level data for CSE meetings |

### Objection Handling

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "It's expensive" | At $41/hr of credentialed instruction, it's 58-79% less than private OG tutoring ($100-200/hr). Plus you get weekly reports and a CSE-ready final report included. |
| "My child doesn't have a formal IEP" | Students with 504 Plans or documented learning differences are welcome. We discuss fit on the discovery call. |
| "How is this different from Kumon/Sylvan?" | Those are generic programs. We read your child's actual IEP, map sessions to their annual goals, and use evidence-based methods (Orton-Gillingham) designed for learning differences. |
| "Can my child handle a group setting?" | Groups are max 6, intentionally grouped by learning profile. If 1:1 is needed, we offer limited individual sessions. |
| "What if I'm not sure about the program?" | Book a free 20-minute discovery call. We'll review your child's situation, discuss fit, and you'll talk to the actual person who runs the program — no sales pressure. |
