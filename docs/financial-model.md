## 4. Pricing Strategy

### Current Pricing

| Program | Price | Per-Week | Per-Hour* |
|---------|-------|----------|----------|
| Reading & Language | $3,500 | $583 | $36.46 |
| Math & Numeracy | $3,500 | $583 | $36.46 |
| Full Academic | $4,000 | $667 | $41.67 |

*Based on 96 instructional hours over 6 weeks (4 hrs/day × 4 days/wk × 6 wks)

### Market Comparison

| Provider | Price | Duration | Per-Week Equivalent |
|----------|-------|----------|-------------------|
| Winston Prep | $4,500 | 4 weeks | $1,125/wk |
| JCC ACES | $5,800–$9,675 | 4-8 weeks | $1,209–$1,450/wk |
| Private OG tutor (1 hr/day) | $2,400–$4,800 | 6 weeks | $400–$800/wk |
| **IEP & Thrive** | **$3,500–$4,000** | **6 weeks** | **$583–$667/wk** |

### Pricing Recommendations

1. **Current pricing is well-positioned.** Below premium competitors, above generic alternatives, strong value proposition.
2. **Early-bird discount:** 10% off for enrollments before April 30 ($3,150 / $3,600). Drives early cash flow.
3. **Sibling discount:** 15% off second child ($2,975 / $3,400). Increases household conversion.
4. **Year 2 price increase:** 7–10% ($3,745–$3,850 / $4,280–$4,400). Market supports annual increases.
5. **Payment plan option:** Split remaining balance into 2 installments (May + June). Reduces friction.
