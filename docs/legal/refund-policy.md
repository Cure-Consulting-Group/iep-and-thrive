## 3. Parent-Initiated Cancellation

| When You Cancel | Deposit | Balance Refund |
|----------------|---------|---------------|
| **30+ days before** program start (before June 7) | Non-refundable — may be applied as credit to a future program or sibling enrollment | 100% of balance paid |

**How to cancel:** Submit cancellation in writing to [contact email]. The date the written cancellation is received determines the refund tier.

## 4. IEP & Thrive-Initiated Cancellation

### Program Cancellation (Full Program)
If IEP & Thrive cancels the program for any reason (e.g., insufficient enrollment, facility unavailability, force majeure), **all deposits and tuition payments will be refunded in full within 14 business days**.

### Student Dismissal
If a student is dismissed from the program due to:
- **Behavioral reasons** (persistent disruption compromising safety/learning of others): A pro-rata credit for unused sessions may be issued at sole discretion. No cash refund after program start.
- **Non-compliance with attendance policy** (3+ unexcused absences): No refund.
- **Failure to disclose material medical/behavioral information:** No refund.

---

## 5. Earned Program Credits

Non-refundable deposits and unexpended tuition may be converted to program credits under the following conditions:
- Credits apply to **future IEP & Thrive programs** (summer, school-year, break intensives) or sibling enrollment
- Credits are **valid for 12 months** from the date of issue
- Credits are **non-transferable** (may not be gifted to another family)
- Credits have **no cash value** and cannot be redeemed for money

---

## 6. Refund Processing

- Approved refunds will be processed to the **original payment method** within **14 business days** of approval
- Stripe processing fees (approximately 2.9% + $0.30) are non-recoverable for completed transactions
- Refunds may take an additional 5–10 business days to appear on your statement depending on your bank
