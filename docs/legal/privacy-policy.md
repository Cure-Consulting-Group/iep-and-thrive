## 1. Introduction

IEP & Thrive ("we," "us," "our") operates the website [iepandthrive.com] and the IEP & Thrive Summer Intensive program. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you visit our website or enroll in our program.

We are committed to protecting the privacy of our students and their families, particularly as we serve minors with Individualized Education Programs (IEPs) and documented learning differences.

---

## 2. Regulatory Framework

This policy is designed to align with the following regulations:

| Regulation | Applicability |
|-----------|---------------|
| **COPPA** (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) | We serve children under 13; parental consent required for data collection |
| **FERPA** (Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act) | We receive education records (IEPs) from parents — FERPA obligations apply when accessing student education records |
| **NY SHIELD Act** | NY-specific data breach notification requirements |
| **CCPA/CPRA** (if CA residents enroll) | California consumer privacy rights |
| **ADA** (Americans with Disabilities Act) | Disability-related data requires heightened protection |

## 6. COPPA Compliance (Children Under 13)

Because our program serves children under 13:

- **We obtain verifiable parental consent** before collecting any personal information about enrolled children
- **We collect only information necessary** for the educational purpose of the program
- **We do not condition participation** on the collection of more information than necessary
- **Parents have the right** to review, correct, or request deletion of their child's information
- **We do not disclose children's information** to third parties other than those necessary to operate the program
- **Parents may revoke consent** at any time by contacting us

---

## 7. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect personal information:

- **Encryption:** Student records stored in encrypted cloud storage
- **Access controls:** Only authorized staff may access student files
- **Secure payments:** Payment processing via Stripe (PCI DSS Level 1 certified)
- **Physical security:** Physical student files stored in locked cabinets at program facility
- **Training:** All staff trained on confidentiality and data handling
- **Incident response:** Breach notification within 72 hours as required by NY SHIELD Act
