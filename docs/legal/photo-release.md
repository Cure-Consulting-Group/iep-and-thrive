## 1. Purpose

This release authorizes IEP & Thrive (a program of Cure Consulting Group LLC) (the "Program") to capture and use photographs and short video recordings of your child during the Summer 2026 intensive (July 7 – August 15, 2026, Long Island, NY).

We capture media for three purposes:

1. **Portfolio artifacts** — photos of your child's work (writing samples, math worksheets, art projects) attached to their weekly progress record. Visible to you in the parent portal. Used internally for the CSE-ready final report.
2. **Lesson documentation** — short videos of structured-literacy lessons or movement blocks. Used internally for instructor self-review and for the family showcase on the final program day.
3. **Marketing & program advocacy** (separate opt-in below) — promotional use on iepandthrive.com, social media, family case studies, and grant/foundation reports. Children are never identified by full name in marketing materials without a separate, written consent.

---

## 2. Scope of Permission

By signing this release, you grant the Program a **non-exclusive, royalty-free, perpetual** license to capture, store, and use the following media of your child:

- Still photographs taken during program hours (9am–1pm, Mon–Fri).
- Short video clips (typically <60 seconds) captured during program hours.
- Photographs of student work product, including handwritten or typed materials.

The Program will **not** capture:

- Footage of your child outside of program hours or off the program premises.
- Audio recordings of your child for any use outside the immediate family showcase.
- Identifying information (full name, school district, IEP details) in any media used outside the parent portal.

---

## 3. Marketing Use — Separate Opt-In

Marketing use (publication on iepandthrive.com, paid or organic social media, written case studies, B2B/district sales decks) requires a **separate, affirmative opt-in** below. You may grant portfolio + lesson use without granting marketing use, and you may withdraw marketing consent at any time without affecting your child's enrollment.

> ☐ **I CONSENT** to use of my child's image and likeness in IEP & Thrive marketing materials (website, social, case studies). My child will not be identified by full name; only first name and grade level may appear.
>
> ☐ **I DO NOT CONSENT** to marketing use. Portfolio and lesson-documentation use only.

---

## 4. Retention & Deletion

- **Portfolio + lesson media:** retained for the duration of the program plus 12 months for instructor record-keeping and CSE-meeting reference.
- **Marketing media (if opted in):** retained for up to 3 years; you may request deletion at any time by emailing hello@iepandthrive.com.
- After the retention period, media is permanently deleted from cloud storage. The signed PDF of this release is retained as part of your child's enrollment record.

---

## 5. Withdrawal of Consent

You may withdraw this consent at any time by emailing hello@iepandthrive.com with your child's name and the subject line "Withdraw Photo Release". On receipt:

- Marketing-use media is removed within 14 days.
- Portfolio media is preserved if its removal would compromise the CSE-ready final report; otherwise removed within 14 days.
- Future media capture for your child stops immediately.

Withdrawal does not retroactively invalidate uses that already occurred (e.g. printed materials already distributed).

---

## 6. No Compensation

You acknowledge that no compensation will be paid for the use of media covered by this release, and that you are signing voluntarily.

## 7. Acknowledgement

By signing below, you confirm:

- You are the legal parent or guardian of the named student and have authority to grant this release.
- You have read and understood Sections 1–6.
- You understand that marketing use is a separate opt-in (Section 3) and that you may decline it without affecting your child's enrollment.
